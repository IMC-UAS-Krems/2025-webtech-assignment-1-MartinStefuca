About me:

I was born on the 6th of November 2005 in the capital of Slovakia, Bratislava. I lived in the city for the first few years of my life, until my parents separated. Afterwards, I was switching between the two.

In my elementary school I have met people that I can call best friends even today. Za kasárňou (the elementary school) has not only given me a solid foundation in my education but also created some of my most memorable memories. Highschool was also great, I have also created lots of friends there and learned to speak English fluently, already in the first year, which helped me massively with what was about to come.

The second year of highschool I didn't visit the same school. My step-father got a job offer in Egypt where we have relocated for 2 years. It was a splendid experience that fostered my english fluency as well as confidance in speaking. People there came from different backgrounds and it was highly interesting to talk to them (especially my friend from North Korea). After the 2 years I have returned to Slovakia and finished my mandatory education there.

Currently, I am studying Informatics, at the IMC University of Applied Sciences in Krems an der Donau.
